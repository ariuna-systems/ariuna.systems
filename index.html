Turning real-time data into real-world decisions

Mission

Transforming how teams in regulated industries see, understand, and respond to complex environments—with live data and autonomous insight.

Platform

Aether is a situational awareness platform that fuses data and intelligence into one commanding view—turning uncertainty into action across physical and digital domains.

  • Throughput of more than 2,500,000 events per second.

Solutions

Situational Awareness

Aether provides a unified view of your data, enabling you to make informed decisions in real-time. With its advanced analytics and visualization capabilities, you can gain insights into your operations like never before.

Data Fusion

Aether's data fusion capabilities allow you to combine data from multiple sources, providing a comprehensive view of your operations. This enables you to identify patterns and trends that would otherwise go unnoticed.

Real-time Analytics

Aether's real-time analytics engine processes data as it arrives, enabling you to act on insights immediately. This is crucial for mission-critical applications where every second counts.

Technology

RinQ

RinQ is a real-time data processing engine that enables organizations to analyze and act on data as it arrives. With its high throughput and low latency, RinQ is designed for mission-critical applications where every second counts.

Custom Intelligence

Empower your teams with the ability to build and integrate custom Python packages and Jupyter notebooks directly into the platform. Whether it’s real-time data analysis, mission-specific algorithms, or machine learning models, your field and command units can deploy tailored logic and automation—right where decisions are made.

Domain-driven Approach

Terminology should align with the target users' existing vocabulary and mental models of data analysis tasks. Understanding the user's perspective is crucial for effective design.
