Proven Capabilities

Automated Tasks & Internal Data Processes

Streamline intricate workflows, minimize manual intervention, and guarantee data integrity across your core operations, ensuring efficiency and compliance.

Digital Twins & Situational Awareness

Real-Time Data/Event Processing

Core Architecture

Kairo Engine

Our core stream processing engine, Kairo is purpose-built for exceptional throughput and low latency. It excels in mission-critical scenarios, processing over 2,500,000 events per second to drive demanding applications and deliver immediate insights.

Robust Storage Engine

A high-performance, secure data repository optimized for storing and retrieving vast quantities of time-series, event, and transactional data at speed, forming the reliable backbone for all analytics and operational processes.

Operations Control Center

Your intuitive central interface for system configuration, real-time data visualization via powerful dashboards, and exercising complete operational control.

Data Integrators & Connectors

Data collection processes designed for effortless integration with your enterprise systems, diverse data sources, third-party applications, and industrial hardware, ensuring a unified and holistic operational view.

Future Vision

At Arjuna, innovation is continuous. We are committed to evolving our platform to keep you at the forefront of operational excellence. Our roadmap is focused on delivering even more sophisticated capabilities built upon the foundation of advanced digital twins.

Advanced Digital Twins & Simulation

Move beyond static models to create dynamic, interactive digital replicas of your entire operational ecosystem. Leverage these twins for powerful simulation capabilities, allowing you to test scenarios, predict outcomes, optimize strategies, and train personnel in a risk-free virtual environment before real-world implementation.

Operations Intelligence & Research

Our vision involves embedding advanced analytics directly into your operational workflows. This empowers proactive anomaly detection to preempt issues, intelligent optimization for peak efficiency and resource use, and comprehensive risk analysis for data-driven mitigation of operational, financial, and compliance threats

Dynamic Planning & Adaptive Scheduling

We aim to implement intelligent systems that can dynamically adjust plans, schedules, and resource allocations in real-time. This will allow your operations to respond fluidly to evolving conditions, unforeseen events, and incoming data streams, maintaining peak efficiency.

Full-Scale SCADA Systems

Looking ahead, our platform will evolve towards comprehensive Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems. This will provide holistic, centralized command and intricate control over your entire interconnected system of assets and processes, offering unparalleled operational oversight. Our inherent agility ensures these advancements are rapidly developed and integrated, guaranteeing you always have access to the most powerful and adaptive tools to thrive in your regulated domain.
